My Favourite Movies

By Philip Drinkwater

My All-Time Best Movies Ever

Apollo 13

As a kid I remember this event happening. However, at the age of 9, I didn't really understand what actually happened. When this film came out 25 years after the event itself, I was engrossed!

Aladdin

One of the first of the 'new generation' of Disney animations that my daughter engaged with (together with The Little Mermaid and Beauty And The Beast).

All Of Us Strangers

A beautifully sad love story of two lonely guys. The film explores the effects of loneliness on mental health with an LGBT+ theme.
